How this becomes the real site

Everything above maps one-to-one to the reference you sent; here's what each piece needs.

Typeface

The reference is set in Aeonik — a commercial face (license ≈ $60–200 for web). Closest free faces on Google Fonts (drop-in via next/font): Instrument Sans, Schibsted Grotesk, or Hanken Grotesk. This mock uses Helvetica Neue as a stand-in.

One face does everything, like the reference: light-tracked medium weight at display size, regular for body, Geist Mono stays for rails/labels.

Palette

#F0EFED ground #161719 ink #8B8D91 dim #FFFFFF chat surface #2E7D46 status only

Warm-gray ground exactly like the reference (not pure white — that's what lets the photo blend). Ink near-black. Green appears only as the twin's "online" status.

The portrait (the one thing only you can do)

For the photo to sit like the reference, shoot it to spec: plain light-gray backdrop (close to #F0EFED — a white wall in soft daylight works), soft directional light from one side, waist-up, camera at eye level, looking into the lens.

Convert to B&W with lifted contrast; export ≥1600px wide. I'll mask the backdrop into the page ground so it bleeds like the reference — that blend is the whole trick.

The twin message bar

Lives in the hero, between the intro line and the scroll cue — the site's front door. At launch (no backend): submitting opens a lightweight answer sheet under the bar fed by curated Q&A from the page's own content, with deep links to sections. The "beta" note keeps it honest.

Phase 3: same bar, live — RAG over your site + résumé + repos, streaming answers with source chips. The UI doesn't change; only the brain does. Fully keyboard-accessible; green dot = twin online.

Below the fold

Sections keep this system: About (short, next to a second crop of the portrait), Projects as a quiet list with big hover previews, Experience, then contact. RAG work always listed first.

Stats stay honest and update as facts change — they're content, not decoration.

SEO follows the positioning

Title/description, keywords, and JSON-LD knowsAbout shift to lead with RAG & agentic AI; the OG image gets regenerated in the new light style. Structured data keeps the same Person/ProfilePage entity — no re-verification needed.
